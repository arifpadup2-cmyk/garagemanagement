# Product

## Register

product

## Users

- **Garage owner/manager** — runs the business from a desk or laptop; creates job cards, invoices, tracks accounts. Needs dense information and fast workflows.
- **Technicians** — on the workshop floor, phones/tablets, bright ambient light, gloves or greasy hands. Log in with a 4-digit PIN, view assigned work, start/complete jobs, add remarks.
- **Service advisors** — front-of-house, customer-facing; attach photos of complaints, manage vehicle intake.

Context: a real auto-service garage in Qatar (Tecido). Screens are often viewed in bright light; touch targets must be generous.

## Product Purpose

End-to-end garage management: vehicle intake → job cards with work items → technician execution → invoicing/payments → accounts (double-entry income/expense). Success = staff complete their daily workflow faster than pen-and-paper, with no lost job cards and accurate money records.

## Brand Personality

Industrial & dependable. Workshop-grade: sturdy, high-contrast, no-nonsense. Feels like a well-organized tool chest — everything where you expect it, nothing decorative getting in the way. Voice: plain, direct, confident.

## Anti-references

- Startup-y SaaS landing aesthetics (gradients, glassmorphism, playful illustrations) — this is a work tool.
- Thin, low-contrast "elegant" gray text — unreadable in a bright workshop.
- Dense enterprise ERP clutter with 40 fields per screen — staff are not accountants.

## Design Principles

1. **Legible under sunlight** — contrast beats subtlety; body text well above WCAG AA.
2. **Glove-friendly** — big tap targets (≥44px), forgiving spacing on interactive controls.
3. **One job per screen** — each view serves one workflow step; no decorative detours.
4. **Money is sacred** — totals, payments, and statuses must be unmistakable at a glance.
5. **Fast on cheap hardware** — single-file app must stay snappy on low-end Android tablets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum; prefer AAA contrast for body text where practical (bright-light environment).
- Touch targets ≥44×44px for all interactive controls used by floor staff.
- Arabic/RTL support is a future requirement (Qatar market) — avoid layout decisions that hard-block RTL.
- Reduced-motion alternatives for any animation.
